Instructions

In this experiment, you will be randomly matched with another person on Amazon Mechanical Turk. Neither of you will ever know the identity of the other. Both of you will receive a fixed amount of $0.25 for participating in the experiment. In addition, you will be able to earn more money based on the decision you make in the experiment.

Each of you will choose whether to COOPERATE or DEFECT in your interaction with the other:

If you both choose to COOPERATE, you will each earn a $0.50 bonus.

If you choose to DEFECT while the other person chooses to COOPERATE, you will earn a $0.75 bonus and the other person will earn no bonus.

If you choose to COOPERATE while the other person chooses to DEFECT, you will earn no bonus and the other person will earn $0.75.

If you both choose to DEFECT, you will each earn a $0.25 cent bonus.
